Welcome


My name is Emily Durling, I'm an artist, illustrator, graphic desiger, and web designer living in North Carolina. I work in a variety of different mediums typically digital or traditional mediums such as watercolor and colored pencil. I have an interest in creating a wide range of different designs, anything from modern to retro in style.
Most of my work experience in the design field involves freelance work for private clients, though I also spend a portion of my time working on personal projects related to concept art and character design, among other things.
